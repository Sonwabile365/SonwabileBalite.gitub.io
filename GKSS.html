The Geekulcha Student Society (GKSS)

The GKSS is a student society that is focused on uplifting the student outside of the classroom. We've had events/talks that were based on: personal branding, How to improve your CV, Money Management as a student, or new industry professional, etc...

As chairperson I had to organize and oversee these events and ensure that they went well. We also competed in hackathons and coding competitions. Prior to this I was the treasurer for the club.
