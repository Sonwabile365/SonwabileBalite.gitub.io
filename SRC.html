The Student Representative Council (SRC)

The Student Representative Council (SRC) is a group of students elected by their peers to represent all students within the school. In the year 2022 I was one of the two acting Vice-Presidents. My duties included assisting in managing the SRC board to ensure that we operate as a 'well-oiled machine'. We organised multiple school events, such as 'school bashes', the first-year orientation, and a lot more.

Unfortunately after a term of serving as one of the Vice-Presidents, I made the extremely difficult decision to step down, as the demands of my position were conflicting with my academics. It was a very difficult decision to make, because of the passion I had for the role and SRC, but it was and remains the best decision I could've taken. Prior to this I had a position in the SRC board in the Student Relations department and duties of our department included communicating effectively between the students and management and representing the students well.
